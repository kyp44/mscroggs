\cproblem{1}{1936}{\card@xxiv@i}{
  Clearly larger factors will produce a larger product.
  It is also obvious that the largest two-digit number that can be produced using the digits $1$ to $4$ is $44$.
  Hence, the largest product is $44 \cdot 44 = 44^2 = 1936$.
}
